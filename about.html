Our Mission

FreeAITools was created with one simple goal: make AI accessible to everyone. Most AI tools today require you to sign up, verify your email, and often pay a monthly fee. We think that is wrong.

We have built a collection of 36 powerful AI tools that you can use instantly, right in your browser, with no login, no signup, and no hidden costs. From paraphrasing and resume building to image compression and QR code generation, everything is free.

What We Offer

Our platform covers a wide range of use cases for students, professionals, freelancers, and everyday users. Whether you need to rewrite a paragraph, convert an image to PDF, generate a business plan, or create a cover letter, FreeAITools has you covered.

All tools run entirely in your browser. We do not store your files, text, or personal data on our servers. Your privacy is important to us.

Our Values

Free Access

All 36 tools are completely free to use. No premium plans, no credit cards, no trials.

Privacy First

We do not collect personal data. Your files and text never leave your device.

No Login Ever

Use any tool instantly. We will never ask you to create an account.

Always Improving

We regularly add new tools and improve existing ones based on user feedback.

2026 FreeAITools - Free forever. No login. No cost.
