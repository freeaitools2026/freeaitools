About FreeAITools

FreeAITools is a free collection of 36 AI-powered tools built to help students, freelancers, creators, and professionals get work done — without logins, without subscriptions, and without hidden costs.

Our mission is simple: make powerful AI tools accessible to everyone, everywhere, for free. Whether you need to paraphrase text, generate a QR code, compress an image, or build a resume — we have a tool for that.

Our tools span 9 categories: writing & text tools, productivity, creativity, health & wellness, finance, learning, coding, image & design, and social media. Every tool works directly in your browser — no installation, no account, no tracking.

Why Free?

We believe AI tools should not be locked behind expensive subscriptions. FreeAITools is supported by advertising, allowing us to keep all 36 tools completely free forever. You get great tools; we get to keep the lights on.
