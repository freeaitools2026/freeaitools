Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Our Commitment

FreeAITools is committed to protecting your privacy. We built this platform with privacy-first principles — your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or sell personal information.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal names, emails, or passwords (no accounts required)
  • Files you upload to our tools (processed locally in your browser)
  • Your text inputs into AI tools
  • Payment or financial information

Information Collected Automatically

Like most websites, we may collect anonymous usage data through:

  • Google Analytics: Anonymous page views and session data to understand how tools are used.
  • Google AdSense: May use cookies to show relevant ads. You can opt out via Google Ad Settings.
  • Standard server logs (IP addresses, browser type) — not linked to personal identity.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies for analytics and advertising purposes only. Our tools themselves do not set cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Third-Party Services

We use Google AdSense for advertising and Google Analytics for anonymous traffic data. These services have their own privacy policies. We do not share data with any other third parties.

Children's Privacy

FreeAITools does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Our tools are designed for general audiences and do not require any registration.
